Why This Blog Exists

Let’s be real: the internet is cluttered with half-baked advice and AI tools that promise the moon but barely reach the gutter. I started CFA to cut through the noise. Here, you’ll get: No-fluff tech guides (think: “How to train ChatGPT on your grandma’s cookie recipes”). Real talk about AI (spoiler: it won’t steal your job… if you use it right). Web design hacks for people who’d rather binge Netflix than debug CSS. This isn’t a lecture hall—it’s a playground. Expect memes, fails, and the occasional existential crisis about robots.
